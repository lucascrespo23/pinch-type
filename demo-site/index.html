Four effects

Pinch Type

Intercepts pinch-to-zoom gestures on mobile and scales text size instead of zooming the page. Text stays in place — just bigger or smaller.

Scroll Morph

A fisheye reading lens: lines near the viewport center render large and bright, lines at the edges shrink and fade. A natural magnifying effect as you scroll.

Combined

Both effects together. The scroll morph fisheye plus pinch-to-zoom scaling. The full experience.

Pinch Lens

A fish-eye lens for text: pinch to magnify words near your fingers while far-away text stays normal. A localized magnifying-glass effect.

Lightweight Mode

Just want pinch-to-zoom on your existing page? The lightweight API works on regular DOM text — no canvas, no dependencies, ~1KB. Vanilla JS or React hook.

Accurate Line Breaking

Text is measured and wrapped using @chenglou/pretext for segment-aware, pixel-perfect line breaking on canvas.
